Founded in 2016 by Lianne McNaughton, who recognized a need within her country for a practical and hands-on means for motivating and empowering youth.

YCDI seeks to engage the youth through workshops, mentorship hands-on and interactive learning sessions with the intent to equip and educate them with the right skills and tools to turn their dreams into reality; changing their self-doubt into unshakable confidence and belief in themselves.

Our Vision

YCDI seeks to equip the youth with the right skills and tools to turn their dreams into reality; changing their self-doubt into unshakable confidence and belief in themselves. Our goal is to impress upon the youth via workshops, mentorship, hands-on and interactive learning initiatives that whatever they imagine can manifest into plausible careers, and can provide solutions to problems they see around them in the community. Most importantly, that they can become the change they wish to see in the world.

Available Workshops

Introduction to Web Development Series

In this 3-part series participants will be introduced to the to web development using HTML (The Hypertext Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) and JavaScript. At the end of this workshop the participants will learn and have developed their own website from scratch. This series does not require any prior knowledge of HTML or web development.
